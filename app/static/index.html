Zero-Sum Dynamics

When one holding group wins a major account, it almost always comes at the expense of another. COMvergence data shows $50.9B in media spend changed hands between the Big 3 across 3,110 account moves. WPP lost $25.5B to Publicis and Omnicom combined, winning only $12.0B back — a $13.5B net deficit.

The Magic Number Is 3

When Publicis hit 3 consecutive net-win quarters, average quarterly return was +24% with +15.5% excess vs S&P 500. COMvergence reveals Publicis is on a 26 consecutive net-win quarter streak (2020-Q1 through 2026-Q2) — they haven't had a single net-loss quarter in over 6 years. WPP's 2025 was devastating: lost Coca-Cola ($805M), Mars ($1.5B), Paramount ($592M), Bayer ($639M) for -$3.4B net. The Estée Lauder consolidation (Q1 2026, from independents/Stagwell/Omnicom) and Wendy's (Q2 2026, $382M from Publicis) start a potential WPP recovery streak.
